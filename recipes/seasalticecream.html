Kingdom Hearts II Sea Salt Ice Cream

Description

Kingdom Hearts is the unlikely Disney crossover RPG that has a serious predilection for neon-teal ice cream bars

Let's start with a gelato al fior di latte (sort of), and see if we can master the art of no-churn popsicles!

Ingridients

  • 1 ½ cup milk
  • 1 ½ cup cream
  • ¾ cup sugar
  • 3 Tbsp cornstarch
  • Packet of gelatin
  • 7 ½ ounces glucose
  • 1 tsp sea salt
  • Teal gel-based food coloring
